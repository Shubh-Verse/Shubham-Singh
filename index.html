Hi I'm Shubham , I am an AI and Machine Learning enthusiast currently pursuing an M.Tech in Artificial Intelligence and Robotics at Gautam Buddha University, with a strong academic background in Computer Science. I have hands-on experience developing AI-powered applications and IoT-based solutions. One of my key projects involved building a deep learning model for dental anomaly detection using EfficientNet, where I worked on data preprocessing, augmentation, and model optimization, achieving 92% classification accuracy. My technical skills include Python, Java, PyTorch, TensorFlow, Scikit-Learn, Flutter, OpenCV, NumPy, and Pandas. I enjoy working at the intersection of AI research and real-world applications, particularly in areas such as AI for healthcare, computer vision, and intelligent IoT systems.
